FoxrishTWOW

FoxrishTWOW is a miniTWOW (Click here if you don't know what that is) I have been hosting since January 2026! As of now, the miniTWOW is currently in its fifthteenth round with the final three contestants: ConvexProjects, MarioFan, and mazuat.

Below you can find a searchable contestant list and explanations of how FoxrishTWOW works, but you can find much more information in the FoxrishTWOW Discord Server and the statistics page! Do know that this page assumes you already know all the basics of how a twow works.

Mechanics


FoxrishTWOW uses a heart system where everyone starts off with 2 fully functioning hearts. The outcome of placing in each of the zones (top 10%, bottom 33%, and bottom 10%) depends on how many / the type of hearts someone has:

If you have two real hearts and no dead hearts If you have one real heart and one dead heart If you have one real heart and one artificial heart
Top 20% Earn a double response prize Dead heart -> artificial heart Earn a double response prize
Bottom 33% One of your real hearts -> dead heart You're eliminated You're eliminated
Bottom 10% You forever lose one of your hearts You're eliminated You're eliminated

On top of the lives system, FoxrishTWOW also has a couple other differences between a standard miniTWOW, including:

  • Extended Limits The word limit is 11 words, allowing the contestants to use slightly more complicated ideas.
  • Snowball Prizing If a contestant places both of their responses in the prize region, they get three responses the next round.
  • Contestant-voted Prompts The prompts are voted on / suggested by the contestants, rather than the host.
